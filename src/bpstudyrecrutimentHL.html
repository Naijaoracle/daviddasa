Kula da Hawan Jini: Inganta Kwarewa

•Kuna da cutar hawan jini?

ko

• Shin kai ma'aikacin lafiya ne da ke da hannu wajen sarrafa hawan jini?

Muna neman mahalarta don binciken bincike don kimanta sabuwar fasaha da ake kira Lifelight don kula da hawan jini.

Nazarin yana nufin fahimtar:

• Yadda marasa lafiya ke samun sauƙi da kwanciyar hankali ta amfani da fasahar Lifelight don auna hawan jini ta amfani da kyamarorin wayoyinsu.

• Ra'ayoyin likitoci game da yuwuwar fa'idodin Lifelight ga marasa lafiya da aikinsu.

• Sha'awar gudanarwa na ɗaukar fasahar Rayuwa.

Idan kai baƙon asibiti ne:

Za a tambaye ku don kammala taƙaitaccen tambayoyin kuma a auna hawan jinin ku ta amfani da na'urar lura da al'ada da fasaha ta Lifelight.

Sannan zaku raba gogewar ku ta amfani da Lifelight ta gajeriyar tambayoyin tambayoyi.

2

Idan kana kiwon lafiya:

Za a tambaye ku don kammala takardar tambaya game da gogewar ku game da fasahar sa ido kan hawan jini da abubuwan da kuka fara gani na Hasken Rayuwa.

Shiga na son rai ne kuma na sirri ne. Duk bayanan za a ɓoye su kafin bincike.

Babu sanannun kasada masu alaƙa da shiga, fa'idodin sun haɗa da samun kyakkyawar fahimtar fasahar Lifelight da rawar da take takawa wajen lura da hawan jini
