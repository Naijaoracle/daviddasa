Welcome to this Chest X-ray Data Analysis

I am excited to share with you a fascinating exploration into the NIH chest x-ray dataset, delve into the wealth of information hidden within medical images. This analysis aims to uncover patterns, understand patient demographics, and shed light on the presence of various medical conditions.

What Was Explored

  1. Data Insights: Started by understanding the basics of the dataset, examining patient ages, and getting an overview of the medical conditions present in the x-rays.
  2. Demographic Patterns: Through visualizations, explored how age groups and gender correlate with the occurrence of medical conditions.
  3. Follow-up Analysis: Understanding the number of follow-ups required for different conditions provides valuable insights into patient care and healthcare planning.
  4. Multi-Condition Analysis: Investigated the co-occurrence of multiple conditions and their distribution across age groups.
