Scenario Template and Authoring Guide

Scenario Management Research Artefact
Scenario Authoring Template

Purpose

This template guides authors in drafting synthetic scenarios for evaluation. It is a human-readable guide, not an executable format.

Scenario Sections

1. Context

  • Setting and care level (e.g., primary care, emergency).
  • Purpose of the scenario.

2. Initial State

  • Patient profile in narrative form.
  • Baseline clinical story and presenting concern.

3. Information Availability

  • What is known at start.
  • What is intentionally unknown or ambiguous.

4. Stressors

  • Time pressure, interruptions, or resource limitations.

5. Objectives

  • Evaluation focus (e.g., safety awareness, communication clarity).

6. Debrief Prompts

  • Questions for reviewers and participants.

Authoring Guidance

  • Keep descriptions narrative and avoid structured schemas.
  • Avoid specifying precise parameter ranges or step-by-step logic.
