Research Artefacts

Methodology and Frameworks for AI-Driven XR Healthcare Simulation

This collection contains conceptual research artefacts that support PhD research on Advanced Non-Player Character (NPC) Systems for XR Healthcare Simulation at Bournemouth University. These documents define evaluation methodologies, assumptions, and frameworks for AI-driven healthcare simulations.

Note: These artefacts are intentionally non-executable and contain no code, schemas, or implementation details. They document research methodology and evaluation frameworks.

Evaluation Framework

Research Context

These artefacts support the DASEX (Diagnosis, Adaptation, Safety, Engagement, eXplainability) evaluation framework for AI-driven healthcare simulations. They provide the methodological foundation for evaluating XR-NPC systems in clinical training environments.

For questions about these research artefacts, contact: ddasa@bournemouth.ac.uk
