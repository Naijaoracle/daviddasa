Ethics and Risk Management Pack

Governance Research Artefact
Governance Ethics Risk Management

Purpose

Provide template language for ethical review and risk management of synthetic studies.

Participant Information Template (Outline)

  • Purpose of the study and synthetic-only boundary.
  • What participants will do during evaluation sessions.
  • Data handling summary (no real patient data).

Consent Language (Outline)

  • Voluntary participation.
  • Right to withdraw at any time.
  • Use of anonymized notes for research reporting.

Risk Assessment Template (Outline)

  • Potential participant burden (time, cognitive load).
  • Mitigations (breaks, debrief, support).
  • Statement of non-clinical use and scope.

Related Artefacts

  • Governance Charter — Overarching governance framework
  • Contribution and Attribution Policy — Research collaboration policies
  • Synthetic Data Principles — Data ethics principles
  • Failure Mode Taxonomy — Risk categories during evaluation
