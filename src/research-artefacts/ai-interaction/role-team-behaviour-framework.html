Role and Team Behaviour Framework

AI Interaction Research Artefact
AI Interaction Teamwork Communication

Purpose

This framework describes roles and communication norms during AI evaluation scenarios. It is written as prose, not executable logic.

Role Definitions (Conceptual)

  • Evaluator Lead: Guides the session, ensures safety boundaries are upheld.
  • Clinical Expert: Assesses clinical plausibility and realism.
  • Simulation Operator: Manages scenario flow and documentation.
  • Observer: Records qualitative observations and evidence.

Communication Patterns

  • Use clear, structured updates at key checkpoints (briefing, mid-point, debrief).
  • Escalate concerns when uncertainty or risk increases.
  • Summarize decisions with rationale, not just outcomes.

Handoff Norms

  • Handoffs include a brief context recap, outstanding questions, and risk flags.
  • Decisions are documented in narrative form with timestamps where relevant.

Boundaries

  • No agent classes or behavior trees are defined.
  • No dialogue system components are implemented.
