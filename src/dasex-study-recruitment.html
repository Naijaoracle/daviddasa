Participant Recruitment – AI-Driven NPC Healthcare Simulation

Full title: Evaluating AI-Driven XR Healthcare Simulation Using the DASEX Framework: A Pilot Validation Study

Ref & Version: 1   |   Ethics ID: 66081   |   Date: Q1 2026

Researcher: David Dasa (PhD Researcher) – ddasa@bournemouth.ac.uk

Supervisor: Professor Wen Tang – wtang@bournemouth.ac.uk

About the Study

We are evaluating an AI-driven Extended Reality (XR) simulation featuring intelligent Non‑Player Characters (NPCs) designed for healthcare training. You'll try a short XR scenario and share your feedback to help improve realism, usability, and training effectiveness.

Eligibility

  • BU Healthcare students (Year 2 and above)
  • Comfortable using a VR headset
  • Able to complete the study session and a 3‑week follow‑up survey

What's involved

  • Orientation to the VR equipment and study overview
  • Complete a short AI‑driven XR simulation scenario
  • Immediate feedback: short checklist and quick survey
  • 3‑week follow‑up: brief JISC survey (by email)

Why participate?

  • Contribute to the next generation of AI‑driven healthcare training
  • Get early access to a cutting‑edge XR simulation
  • Your feedback directly shapes realism, usability, and learning impact

Location, Dates and Duration

  • Location: Bournemouth Gateway Building, Lansdowne Campus
  • Dates: Q1 2026
  • Session Duration: 30 minutes (approx.)

Thank‑you gift

£5 Amazon e‑gift card after each stage (£10 total).

Ready to take part?

Please drop a mail to ddasa@bournemouth.ac.uk if you would like to participate, and we will set up a session at your earliest convenience.
